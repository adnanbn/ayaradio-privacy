# Aya Radio Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Aya Radio is built to provide religious and educational audio programming. We keep data collection minimal and do not use it to identify you.

## What we collect
- **Location (optional)**: Used to calculate accurate prayer times for your area.
- **Device information**: Basic device and OS details to keep the app working reliably.

## How we use it
- To show prayer times based on your location.
- To keep streaming and notifications working properly.

## What we do not do
- We do **not** collect any Personally Identifying Information (PII) such as names, emails, or account data.
- We do **not** sell or share personal data.
- We do **not** track you across other apps or websites.

## Your choices
- You can deny or revoke location access in your device settings.
- If location is disabled, the app uses a default city schedule which will affect streaming time and result in accurate prayer call.

If you have questions, contact us at: info@ayaradio.com
